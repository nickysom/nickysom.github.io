About Me

Hello there! My name is Nicky and I am a Penn State student with a passion for Information Technology. My career aspirations are to become an IT manager and potentially a post-secondary education teacher. I love keeping up with the latest tech, learning new hobbies, and learning new things. Feel free to reach out to me to connect or with any questions!
